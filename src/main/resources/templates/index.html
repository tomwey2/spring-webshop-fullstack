Welcome

This is my reference implementation of a little webshop in order to demonstrate how spring boot works.

Feel free to test my implementation. You can authorize as follows:

  • with your own account: please register, fill the form with your email address and a password. After an order you get a confirmation email in order to show this functionality. If you log out then your account will be deleted again.
  • with one of the fixed implemented demo accounts: please sign in either as 'john.doe@test.com' or 'jane.doe@test.com'. The password is 'password'. In this case, the system does not send an email when finishing the order.

You are welcome to send me any comments. You find the contact information at the information page (link at bottom left of this side).

Thank you for your interest!
